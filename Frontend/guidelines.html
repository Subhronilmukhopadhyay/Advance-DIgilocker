Guidelines for Indian Voters

  • Landing Page: Select "Indian" to proceed as an Indian voter.
  • Home Page: Choose to log in via DigiLocker (using mobile number, username, or Aadhaar with a 6-digit PIN) or Voter ID (using EPIC number and OTP sent to your registered mobile number).
  • Voter Details: After logging in, verify your displayed details (name, Voter ID, DOB, constituency, etc.).
  • Face Detection: Capture a real-time image for liveness verification to ensure authenticity.
  • Voting: View the list of candidates in your constituency (with names, party symbols, and photos). Select your preferred candidate and confirm your choice.
  • Secure Submission: Your vote will be securely stored for election results.

Note: Log in only during your assigned time slot. For assistance, refer to the Election Commission’s header/footer links or contact support.

NRI Voter Guidelines

Here are the guidelines for NRI voters:

  • Ensure you are registered as an NRI voter.
  • Submit Form 6A to the Electoral Registration Officer.
  • You can vote in person or by proxy.
  • Follow the instructions given by polling officers.
