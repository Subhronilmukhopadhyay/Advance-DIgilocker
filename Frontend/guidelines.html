NRI Voter Guidelines

Here are the guidelines for NRI voters:

  • Ensure you are registered as an NRI voter.
  • Submit Form 6A to the Electoral Registration Officer.
  • You can vote in person or by proxy.
  • Follow the instructions given by polling officers.

Indian Voter Guidelines

Here are the guidelines for Indian voters:

  • Ensure you are registered to vote.
  • Carry a valid ID proof to the polling station.
  • Follow the instructions given by polling officers.
  • Respect the secrecy of your vote.
