NRI Voting Registration

Guidelines for Filling Up Form-6A

  1. Every Indian citizen staying abroad, who hasn't acquired foreign citizenship and is 18 years or older as of January 1st of the year, can apply using Form 6A.
  2. The application must be submitted to the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO) of the constituency where the applicant's residence in India is located as per the passport.
  3. Submit the application directly to the ERO in person or by post. Find ERO details on the Election Commission website.
  4. Attach a recent passport-size photograph with a light background (preferably white).
  5. If sent by post, attach attested copies of passport pages and visa details. If submitted in person, bring originals for verification.
  6. Voting requires physical presence at the polling station with the original passport.
